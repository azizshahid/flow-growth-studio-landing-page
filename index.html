The problem

Google Isn't Finding You — and Neither Are Your Customers

Most small business websites share the same silent killers. The frustrating part? They're completely fixable.

Invisible to Search Engines

Technical issues like broken crawl paths, missing meta tags, or slow load times stop Google from even indexing your pages properly — so you're simply not in the game.

Targeting the Wrong Keywords

You might rank for terms nobody searches, while your ideal customers type different phrases and land on a competitor's page instead. Keyword misalignment is the most invisible loss.

Weak On-Page Structure

Poor heading hierarchy, thin content, and unoptimised title tags signal to Google that your pages aren't authoritative enough to earn a top-3 spot.

Slow Load Speeds

Google's Core Web Vitals penalise slow sites. Uncompressed images and bloated code silently push you down the rankings every single day you don't fix them.

Businesses on page 2 receive less than 1% of all clicks. Page one or invisible — there is no middle ground.
